Push Notifications

meteocool can notify you about incoming rain at your current location. The prediction service is based on models provided by DWD. Notification set for the distant future (in weather prediction terms: more than 15 minutes) may be inaccurate, especially for locally developing thunderstorms (common in the summer).

This feature obviously requires you to provide us with your current location. No other information besides your location data, accuracy and an anonymous, random token associated with your web browser will be processed.
