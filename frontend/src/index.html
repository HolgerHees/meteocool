iOS and Android Apps

Accurate Push Notifications

meteocool can notify you about incoming rain or snow at your current whereabouts. Your phone automatically recognizes location changes and starts monitoring that location — no interaction necessary!

The prediction is based on the extremely accurate nowcast models provided by DWD (up to 120 minutes into the future).

Concise Weather Radar

Our concise and beautiful weather radar supports you in spotting and classifying severe weather situations by aggregating data from multiple meteorological sources.

Stay dry or get into the eye of the storm — that's up to you.

Completely Free on iOS and Android
The official meteocool App is available on the Apple App Store. The Android App is currently being beta-tested.

Please note that some of the latest features might not yet be available on Android. If you'd like to change that, we're looking for contributors.
